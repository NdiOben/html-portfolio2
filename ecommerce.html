Challenges & Solutions

Responsive Product Grid

Creating a product grid that works seamlessly across all devices was challenging due to varying product image sizes.

Solution: Implemented CSS Grid with aspect-ratio boxes and object-fit to ensure consistent display across devices.

Product Filtering

The client needed complex filtering options (price range, categories, colors) without page reloads.

Solution: Developed a JavaScript filtering system that updates results in real-time while maintaining clean URLs for SEO.
